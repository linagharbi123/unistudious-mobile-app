# Privacy Policy for Unistudious

**Effective Date:** November 21, 2024  
**Last Updated:** November 21, 2024

---

Welcome to Unistudious! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application, website, and services.

**Note:** This privacy policy applies to our mobile application available on iOS and Android platforms, as well as our web services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our services:

### 1.1 Identifiers

- **User ID:** Unique account identifier associated with your user profile
- **Device ID:** Unique device identifier used for push notifications and device management
- **Authentication Tokens:** Tokens used for authentication via Google Sign-In, Facebook, and Sign in with Apple

### 1.2 Contact Information

- **Email Address:** Required for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Phone Number:** Optional information collected for account verification and user support

### 1.3 Personal Information

- **Name:** Full name for your user profile
- **Date of Birth:** Collected for account registration and age verification
- **Gender:** Optional information for profile customization
- **Birth Place:** Optional information for profile completion
- **Address:** Physical address information (optional)
- **Profile Information:** Profile picture, "About Me" section, and other profile-related data

### 1.4 User Content

- **Photos:** Images uploaded for courses, messages, and profile pictures
- **Videos:** Video content uploaded for courses and messages
- **Audio Data:** Voice messages, audio recordings, and audio content shared through the app
- **Text Messages:** Private and group messages sent through the chat and messaging features
- **Files and Documents:** PDF files, documents, and other files shared through the platform
- **Other User Content:** Any other content you create, upload, or share through the application

### 1.5 Usage Data

- **Product Interaction:** Pages visited, features used, buttons clicked, and interactions with the app
- **Session Data:** Active sessions, time spent in the app, and usage patterns
- **Other Usage Data:** General usage statistics and analytics data
- **Push Notification Preferences:** Your notification settings and preferences

### 1.6 Diagnostic Data

- **Crash Data:** Crash reports, error logs, and exception information collected via Firebase Crashlytics
- **Performance Data:** App performance metrics, load times, and performance monitoring data collected via Firebase Performance
- **Other Diagnostic Data:** Additional diagnostic information for troubleshooting and improving app stability

### 1.7 Device Information

- **Device Type:** Type of device (iPhone, iPad, Android phone, etc.)
- **Operating System:** iOS or Android version
- **App Version:** Version of the Unistudious application you are using
- **Browser Information:** Browser type and version (for web services)

### 1.8 Location Information

- **IP Address:** Automatically collected when you use our services
- **Coarse Location:** Approximate geographic location derived from your IP address (not precise GPS location)

### 1.9 Financial Information (If Applicable)

- **Payment Information:** Billing address, payment method details, and transaction information (if payment features are enabled)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

- **App Functionality:** To provide and improve our online learning platform services
- **Account Management:** To manage your account, authenticate your identity, and maintain your user profile
- **Communication:** To facilitate communication between students and educators through messaging and chat features
- **Push Notifications:** To send you notifications about courses, messages, updates, and important information
- **Personalization:** To personalize your learning experience and provide relevant content recommendations
- **Analytics:** To analyze app usage, improve our services, and understand user behavior
- **Security:** To ensure security, prevent fraud, and protect against unauthorized access
- **Developer's Advertising or Marketing:** To send you marketing communications and promotional materials (with your consent)
- **Support:** To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with the following third parties:

### 3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

**Firebase (Google):**
- For user authentication and account management
- For push notifications and device management
- For analytics and usage tracking
- For crash reporting via Firebase Crashlytics
- For performance monitoring via Firebase Performance

**Google Sign-In:** For authentication via Google account

**Facebook:** For authentication via Facebook account

**Apple:** For authentication via Sign in with Apple

**Rocket.Chat:**
- For real-time messaging and chat services
- For storing and transmitting messages, photos, videos, and audio data
- For group chat functionality

**Jitsi Meet:**
- For video meetings and online sessions
- For audio and video communication during live sessions

**Backend Server:**
- For storing user data, course content, and files
- For managing user profiles and account information
- For processing and managing course materials

**Payment Processors:** For processing payments and transactions (if payment features are enabled)

### 3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, legal process, or to enforce our terms and policies.

### 3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

## 4. Data Collection and Storage Details

### 4.1 Data Linked to Your Identity

All data types mentioned above are linked to your user identity and associated with your account.

### 4.2 Data Not Used for Tracking

We do not use your data for cross-app advertising tracking or to track you across other apps or websites.

### 4.3 Data Sharing

As detailed in Section 3, we share certain data types with third-party service providers to enable app functionality. Phone numbers, date of birth, and gender are not shared with third parties and are stored only on our backend server.

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct your personal information through the app settings
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Opt-out:** Disable push notifications in your device or app settings
- **Restrict:** Limit the processing of your data
- **Portability:** Request transfer of your data to another service
- **Object:** Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 12.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption:** SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- **Secure Storage:** Secure storage of passwords and authentication tokens
- **Access Controls:** Restricted access to sensitive information
- **Security Audits:** Regular security audits and assessments
- **Firebase Security:** Use of Firebase security features for data protection

**Important Note:** No system is completely secure. We encourage you to protect your account credentials and never share them with others.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

- Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.
